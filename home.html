        Functional Foods

Unlocking the Medicine in Foods

My books have been written on foods and their nutritive values while even many more exist on health and wellness. However, M the connection between food and health is often glossed over or at best limited to the consumption of some foods as risk factors for some diseases. However, while it's true that some foods can kill, it is also true that some foods can heal- these are called functional foods.

This book focuses on the concept of functional food as key to unlocking the medicine in food. It has been richly packed with various topics that has been carefully divided into sections including, role of functional foods in optimizing health care delivery in developing world and mechanisms of action of food bioactive components. The aspect of Consumer acceptability of functional foods and nutraceuticals was also well considered in this book, as well as increasing awareness on biodiversity and conservation of plant and animal resources as functional foods and nutraceuticals.

In view of the fact that the field of functional food research is gradually evolving and lots of technological advancements are being experienced in order to foster developments of functional food products at industrial scale, this book also contain sections on biotechnology as a vital tool for bridging the gap between functional food research and industrial utilization, and functional food product development and regulations.

The aspect of nutrigenomics which is the emerging field of 'personalized nutritional therapy' was not also left out. Therefore, this book offers a lot for everyone including the nutritionists, biochemists, food scientists, biotechnologists, agriculturists, conservationist, entrepreneurs, industrialists and policy makers
